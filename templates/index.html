Patient Information

Clinical Metrics

👤 Your current age in whole years. Higher age increases diabetes risk.
💧 Blood sugar level after 8-hour fast (mg/dL). Get from doctor or home glucose meter. Normal: 70-100, Prediabetes: 100-125, Diabetes: 126+
⚖️ BMI = Weight(kg) ÷ Height(m)². Calculate online or ask doctor. Normal: 18.5-24.9, Overweight: 25-29.9, Obese: 30+
❤️ Diastolic blood pressure (lower number, mm Hg). Measure at doctor's office or pharmacy. Normal: <80, Elevated: 80-89, High: 90+
👶 Total number of pregnancies (for females). Leave as 0 if not applicable. Used to identify gestational diabetes risk.
📏 Triceps skin fold thickness (mm). Measured at doctor's office using calipers. Reflects body fat percentage. Leave as 20 if unknown.
💉 2-hour serum insulin after glucose test (mu U/ml). Get from doctor after glucose tolerance test. Indicates insulin resistance. Normal: 12-150.
🧬 Family history of diabetes scoring (genetic risk factor). Higher = more relatives with diabetes. Get from doctor or assess family history. Use 0.47 if unsure.

About CodeCure

An AI-driven health-tech platform designed to enhance healthcare delivery, patient engagement, and health literacy through intelligent machine learning systems.

Architecture

Built with FastAPI backend, Scikit-learn AI engine, SQLite database, and a premium HTML/CSS/JS frontend. Designed for scalability from MVP to production.

AI Models

Three models are trained and evaluated — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting. The best performer is automatically selected and deployed.

Dataset

Trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset distribution — featuring 8 clinical features including glucose, BMI, blood pressure, insulin, and genetic factors.

Privacy & Compliance

Designed with HIPAA and India's DPDP Act in mind. Local-first data storage, no external data sharing, and role-based access architecture.

Roadmap

Next: JWT authentication, Heart disease prediction, AI symptom checker (LLM-based), Report PDF upload & summary, Doctor dashboard, and cloud deployment.

Mission

Build an AI Chronic Care Management Platform for India, tackling diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease — conditions that affect hundreds of millions.

Tech Stack

Python FastAPI Scikit-learn SQLAlchemy SQLite Jinja2 NumPy Pandas

Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly through the AI diagnostic forms, including age, glucose levels, and other health metrics. We also use a device-specific identifier to track your prediction history.

2. Data Usage

The collected data is used specifically to provide AI-driven health risk predictions and to improve our machine learning models. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device for quick access, while clinical metrics may be processed on our secure servers to generate analysis results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about CodeCure

What is CodeCure?

CodeCure is an advanced AI health-tech platform that uses Machine Learning to provide predictive insights into chronic conditions like diabetes.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our models achieve high accuracy on clinical datasets. However, AI predictions are probabilistic and should only be used as a preliminary screening tool, not a final diagnosis.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use secure transmission and storage protocols. Most of your browsing history stays locally on your device.

Can I use this for clinical decisions?

No. You must consult a certified medical professional for any clinical decisions or treatments.

How can I get my report?

After running an AI analysis, a "Download Report" button will appear. You can download a high-quality PDF containing your scores and recommendations.

Why does my score change?

Your Health Score is dynamic. It reflects the latest data you provide. Changes in glucose levels, BMI, or blood pressure will directly influence your calculated index.

How do I talk to the AI?

Use the chat bubble icon in the bottom right corner to open the CodeCure AI Assistant for instant help with platform queries.

Documentation

Technical details and usage guide

Platform Overview

CodeCure is built using a modern stack (Python, FastAPI, Scikit-learn) to bridge the gap between AI research and clinical utility.

How it Works

  1. Data Input: Users provide vitals and medical metrics.
  2. AI Analysis: Our Random Forest and Neural models process the data.
  3. Explainable AI: The system explains which factors (like BMI or Glucose) impacted the score.
  4. Reporting: Users can download a detailed PDF report of their scores.

Technical Architecture

  • Backend: FastAPI for high-performance async processing.
  • Frontend: Vanilla JS with a custom CSS design system.
  • Database: SQLite for local persistence, Vercel Postgres for production.
