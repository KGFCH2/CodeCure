Patient Information

Clinical Metrics

Your age in whole years. Older age increases diabetes risk.
Blood sugar level after 8 hours without eating (mg/dL). Get from doctor or home glucose meter. Normal: 70-100, Pre-diabetes: 100-125, Diabetes: 126+
BMI = Weight(kg) ÷ Height(m)². Calculate online or ask doctor. Normal: 18.5-24.9, Overweight: 25-29.9, Obese: 30+
Bottom blood pressure number (mm Hg). Measure at doctor or pharmacy. Normal: Less than 80, Elevated: 80-89, High: 90+
Total number of pregnancies (for females). Use 0 if not applicable. Helps identify pregnancy-related diabetes risk.
Skin fold thickness measured at the back of arm (mm). Measured at doctor using special tool. Shows body fat level. Use 20 if you don't know.
Insulin level 2 hours after glucose test (mu U/ml). Get from doctor after glucose test. Shows insulin resistance. Normal: 12-150.
Family diabetes history score (genetic risk). Higher = more family members have diabetes. Ask your doctor or check family history. Use 0.47 if unsure.

About CodeCure

An AI health app made to improve doctor care, help patients get better health, and teach people about health using smart machine learning.

Architecture

Built with FastAPI backend, Scikit-learn AI engine, SQLite database, and premium HTML/CSS/JS frontend. Designed to grow from start to large scale.

AI Models

Three models are trained and evaluated — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting. The best performer is automatically selected and deployed.

Dataset

Trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset distribution — featuring 8 clinical features including glucose, BMI, blood pressure, insulin, and genetic factors.

Privacy & Compliance

Designed with healthcare laws in mind (HIPAA and India's DPDP Act). Data stays on your device except when needed for analysis. Clear access controls for different users.

Roadmap

Next: JWT authentication, Heart disease prediction, AI symptom checker (LLM-based), Report PDF upload & summary, Doctor dashboard, and cloud deployment.

Mission

Build an AI Chronic Care Management Platform for India, tackling diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease — conditions that affect hundreds of millions.

Tech Stack

Python FastAPI Scikit-learn SQLAlchemy SQLite Jinja2 NumPy Pandas

Documentation

Technical details and usage guide

CodeCure is built using a modern stack (Python, FastAPI, Scikit-learn, HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS) to bridge the gap between AI research and clinical utility. The platform combines cutting-edge machine learning with an intuitive user interface designed for ease of use.

  1. Data Input: Users provide vitals and medical metrics through the simple prediction form.
  2. AI Analysis: Our Random Forest and Neural Network models process the data in real-time.
  3. Results Display: The system shows which factors (BMI, Glucose, etc.) most impacted your score.
  4. Reporting: Users can download a detailed, professional PDF report of their scores and recommendations.
  • Backend: FastAPI for fast async processing, Render deployment, 750 hours/month free tier.
  • Frontend: Vanilla JS with custom CSS design system, deployed on Vercel with unlimited free deployments.
  • Database: SQLite for local development, supports Vercel Postgres for production scaling.
  • ML Models: Scikit-Learn Random Forest (95%+ accuracy) and Neural Networks for robust predictions.

Prediction Endpoint: POST /api/predict - Submit health metrics and receive AI risk assessment

Dashboard Endpoint: GET /api/dashboard - Retrieve prediction history and analytics

Health Check: GET /api/health - Verify backend service status

Random Forest Classifier: Primary model for diabetes risk prediction. Trained on comprehensive medical datasets. Provides feature importance rankings showing which metrics most influence risk assessment.

Neural Network: Secondary model for validation and ensemble predictions. Provides probabilistic outputs and confidence intervals for predictions.

CodeCure implements HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Personal health information is stored securely with industry-standard encryption. Local browser storage is used wherever possible to minimize server-side data retention. CORS protection prevents unauthorized API access.
