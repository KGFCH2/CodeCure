About CodeCure

An AI-driven health-tech platform designed to enhance healthcare delivery, patient engagement, and health literacy through intelligent machine learning systems.

Architecture

Built with FastAPI backend, Scikit-learn AI engine, SQLite database, and a premium HTML/CSS/JS frontend. Designed for scalability from MVP to production.

AI Models

Three models are trained and evaluated — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting. The best performer is automatically selected and deployed.

Dataset

Trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset distribution — featuring 8 clinical features including glucose, BMI, blood pressure, insulin, and genetic factors.

Privacy & Compliance

Designed with HIPAA and India's DPDP Act in mind. Local-first data storage, no external data sharing, and role-based access architecture.

Roadmap

Next: JWT authentication, Heart disease prediction, AI symptom checker (LLM-based), Report PDF upload & summary, Doctor dashboard, and cloud deployment.

Mission

Build an AI Chronic Care Management Platform for India, tackling diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease — conditions that affect hundreds of millions.

Tech Stack

Python FastAPI Scikit-learn SQLAlchemy SQLite Jinja2 NumPy Pandas
